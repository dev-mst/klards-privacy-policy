Privacy Policy for Klards

Last updated: July 18, 2025

Overview

Klards ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Progress: Your game statistics, achievements, and settings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app usage data
  • Google Play Games: When you sign in to Google Play Games, we access your Google Play profile and game data

Information You Provide

  • Cloud Save Data: Game progress and settings stored on Google Play Games Services
  • Language Preferences: Your selected language setting

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Track achievements and statistics
  • Provide cloud save functionality
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Comply with Google Play policies

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Play Games Services: For cloud save and achievement functionality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Save: Your game data is securely stored on Google's servers through Google Play Games Services
  • Local Storage: Game settings and progress are stored locally on your device
  • Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data

Children's Privacy

Klards is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Services

Our game integrates with:

  • Google Play Games Services: For cloud save, achievements, and leaderboards
  • Google Play Store: For app distribution and updates

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about your stored data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your game data
  • Opt-out: Disable cloud save functionality in game settings
  • Sign Out: Disconnect from Google Play Games Services

Data Retention

  • Game Progress: Retained until you delete the app or request deletion
  • Cloud Save: Retained according to Google Play Games Services policies
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for improvement purposes

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app or on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: dev.mst.apps@gmail.com
  • Google Play Store: Through the Klards app listing

Your Consent

By using Klards, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to our collection and use of information as described above.

Technical Details (for transparency)

Data Types Collected:

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, play time)
  • Game settings (AI difficulty, visual preferences)
  • Achievement progress
  • Device language setting
  • Google Play Games profile (when signed in)

Data Not Collected:

  • Personal contact information
  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Payment information (no in-app purchases)

Local vs Cloud Data:

  • Local Only: Game settings, temporary progress
  • Cloud Sync: Game statistics, achievements, saved games (only when signed in to Google Play Games)

This policy is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play requirements.
